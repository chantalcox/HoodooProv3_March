Case Study

Echoes

We are startup business generalists. We understand that many challenges and complexities arise at anytime.
We adapt our solution to the present and future needs of Echoes.

Design

We built a new design around the value proposition we came up with. We designed the landing page, the app, and the pitch deck slides.

Marketing

We built the brand, social media strategies and partnerships. We proposed a go-to-action plan after building personas and use cases, and conducting a customer survey.

Metrics

Echoes was not attracting customers. We added the right analytics tools and tracked new KPIs. We A/B tested the site and provided solutions to get customers through the acquisition funnel.

Market

Echoes was focusing on the wrong market. We analyzed the opportunity and placed a go-to-market strategy using an extensive SWOT, competitors analysis and Porter's five forces analysis.

Technology

To make the product more appealing, we provided an extra layer of value with a technical due diligence. We added some key technical aspects including big data and recommender system.

Investment

Echoes was not ready to get investment. We provided a complete pitch desk with all the key visions and contents, and introduced them to investors to make them investment-ready.
